Lori Leonard

Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner Adult Gerontology Nurse Practitioner

Lori is dual board certified as a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner and an Adult Gerontology Nurse Practitioner.

She is certified as a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse practitioner (PMHNP) by the American Nurses Credentialing Center (ANCC) and an Adult - Gerontology Nurse Practitioner by American Academy of Nurse Practitioners (AANP). Her nursing education began at Georgia Highland and she continued on to pursue a bachelor’s degree in nursing at the University of West Georgia, and later she received a Master’s in Nursing at University of Alabama at Birmingham in 2014 & completed a second Master's in Nursing in 2020.

Why Mindset Body Reset?
