How do I Book an appointment?

To make an appointment, please use the contact you to schedule your online appointment. You will be contacted by staff during regular hours to confirm the appointment.

Do I need a referral?

While it is ideal to have a referral from another provider, it is not necessary. Prescreening forms are included in the request for the appointment to assist in meeting your needs.

How do I access my portal?

Once an appointment date has been confirmed, you will receive an email with portal access instructions to complete prior to initial evaluation.

What is your cancellation policy?

In the event that you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, we ask that you give us 48 hours' notice. All late cancellations are subject to a fee.

Do you take insurance?

We're sorry, but we don't accept insurance as payment. We place the utmost importance on the quality & efficiency of our services. Our prices are reflective of this in our care.

Can I get refills?

During the course of treatment, it is your responsibility to manage appointments in a timely manner to avoiding running out of medication. Refills for medications will NOT be provided without a regularly scheduled appointment.
